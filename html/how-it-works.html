How it works !!

Chores app automatically generates weekly tasks with points assigned to it based on how you set it up.

The tasks can be different every week, some people can be excluded from certain chores, other people can have more chores than others and so on.

The schedule is automatically generated for you rather than you need to manually assign chores to people.

At the end of the week a score is calculated and you will have the winner of the week to celebrate.
